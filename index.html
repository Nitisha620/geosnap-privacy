Privacy Policy GeoSnap collects and uses the following data solely to provide core app functionality: • Camera access – used to record videos initiated by the user. • Microphone access – used to record audio with video. • Location access – used to attach GPS coordinates to recordings. • Storage access – used to save recordings locally and upload them to cloud storage. Data Collection: The app does not collect personal data for advertising or analytics. All recorded content is created voluntarily by the user. Data Sharing: Recorded files may be uploaded to a private cloud storage bucket owned by the organization. No data is shared with third parties. Data Security: All uploads occur over secure HTTPS connections. Contact: admin@cognecto.com
